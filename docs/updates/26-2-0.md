# Bedrock Perfected 26.2

::: info
Released on February 1, 2026.
:::

![Version 26.2](/src/updates/26-2.webp)

Hello everyone! Here is the **monthly updates of Bedrock Perfected**.

The current version is bringing some major tweaks into **textures and UI**, and also bringing **new mechanics** to enhance your wandering journey!

# **Below are what's new on version 26.2:**

## Biome Notifier
You will be notified on what biome you're currently in whenever you cross into a new biome.

![biome-notifier](/src/images/new-mechanics/biome-notifier.webp)

This notifier will also work on custom biomes from other add-ons.

::: info
Shot with **[Biomes and Beyond](https://mcpedl.com/biomes-and-beyond/)** add-on by **iKorbon**.
:::

![biome-notifier](/src/images/new-mechanics/bn-bab.webp)

## Brewing Guide UI
Adds a cheat sheet to the Brewing Stand interface.

![Brewing Guide UI](/src/images/better-ui/brewing-guide-ui.webp)

## Show All Trades
Makes all locked trades visibles and allows to see the locked item names and enchantments.

![Dark UI](/src/images/better-ui/show-all-trades.webp)

## Quick Crafting
Adds a button to stack craft items in the inventory, crafting table, stonecutter, and trade faster with villagers!

![Quick Crafting](/src/images/better-ui/quick-crafting.webp)

## Clearer Wither Hearts
Makes Hearts more visible when under the 'Wither' effect.
