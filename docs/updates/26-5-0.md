# Bedrock Perfected 26.5

::: info
Released on May 1, 2026.

![Version 26.5](/src/updates/26-5-0.webp)

Hello hello hellooo! The 26.5 update is here, focusing on adding a bunch of **New Structures**!

This update adds various small-scale structures to make the world feel more lived-in and active while keeping the **Vanilla+** feels.

## New Structures

### Carriage
Carriages can now be found throughout your wandering journey, adding a sense of travel and commerce to the world.

![Big Carriage](/src/images/new-structures/big-carriage.webp)

![Small Carriage](/src/images/new-structures/small-carriage.webp)
